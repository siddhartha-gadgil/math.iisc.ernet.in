Event Title:

Approximation theoretic characterization of finite dimensional normed linear spaces

Speaker:

Dr. D. Narayana

Affiliation:

Department of Mathematics
Indian Institute of Science

Abstract/Brief Description:

We characterize finite dimensional normed linear space as strongly proximinal subspace in all its superspaces. Connection between upper Hausdorff semicontinuity of metric projection and finite dimensionality of subspace is discussed
