Event Title: Optimum experimental design
Speaker: Prof. Volker Schulz
Affiliation: FB IV - Mathematics, University of Trier, 54286 Trier, Germany
Abstract/Brief Description: Often, the measurement process does not yield a sufficiently trustworthy estimation of model parameters. In most cases, the reason is not a lack of efficient numerical PI techniques, but rather a lack of statistical quality of the mapping from parameters to measurement. In this talk, we will discuss this problem and present practical examples of nonlinear optimum experimental design solutions.
Subject Area: Mathematics
