Interview Process


Candidates called for interview are requested to be present at the venue as per the details mentioned in the interview call letter. There will be two interview committees, one interview committee for applicants interested in mathematical engineering and another for those interested in mathematical biology. Faculty members interested in taking students will display their list of projects/research areas. The candidate has to select projects that she/he is interested in. Based on the order of preference, the candidate is interviewed by one or both the committees. After the interviews, the final list of candidates recommended for admission will be displayed on the website.
