  • Algorithmic Challenges of Next Generation Sequencing

  • Phylogenetics and Genome Rearrangements

  • Systems Modeling in Life Sciences

  • Algorithms for Network Modeling, Inference, and Analyses

  • Graph Algorithms in Bioinformatics and Systems Biology

  • Challenges and Opportunities in Clinical Data Analysis

  • Deciphering Intracellular Networks in Mammalian Cells
