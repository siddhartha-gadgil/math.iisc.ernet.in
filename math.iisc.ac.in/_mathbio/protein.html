Understanding protein folding and unfolding landscape

Research Group

Prabal K Maiti and Biman Bagchi

Department of Physics, IISc and SSUC, IISc

A detailed understanding of energy landscapes of proteins is important not only to understand the folding and unfolding transitions, but also essential to correlate conformational motions with functions of enzymes. We propose to explore the energy landscape by performing pulling experiments on several proteins. Initial studies have shown one can indeed construct the folding funnel and the unfolding landscape, with experiments on ubiquitin. We now plan to explore the relative role of alpha helix and beta sheet in protein folding and unfolding. Also planned are studies by adding a co-solvent like ethanol and dimethyl sulphoxide.
