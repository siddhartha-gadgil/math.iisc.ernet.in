Welcome to Mathematics Information Resource Centre!


The Mathematics Informaton Resource Centre (MIRC) collection comprises works on Pure Mathematics (Mathematics & Statistics), Applied Mathematics (including Mathematical Finance, Mathematical Computing, Engineering Mathematics, etc.), Mathematics Education, and Mathematical Biology.

The Information Resource Centre aims to convey information on the primary (i.e. printed & electronic books , journals & proceedings) and secondary (i.e. dictionaries, handbooks, encyclopedias, histories, collected papers of individual mathematicians, etc.) literature in the Mathematics Library collection, and to provide links to various Reference and Information Services for the users of the Information Resource Centre.

This gateway helps you access various types of bibliographic information pertinent to Mathematics and other related fields. The Mathematics Information Resource Centre works together with the JRD Tata Memorial Library to bring you these services.
