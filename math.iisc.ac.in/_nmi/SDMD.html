STATIC AND DYNAMIC MECHANISM DESIGN

 

August 01 - 04, 2015

Nature of the program:


Background lectures on specific topics in static and dynamic mechanism design in the forenoons and early afternoons. One session of research paper presentations by researchers (students strongly encouraged) in the later afternoons.


Topics to be covered:


  • Auction design - private and interdependent values
  • Voting
  • Principles of mechanism design
  • Basics of dynamic mechanism design
  • Market design

    • We welcome Masters and Phd students (all those interested in game theory) and Faculty members.


    There will be limited opportunities for original research paper presentations by Ph.D. students and younger scholars. Those interested in presenting may write directly to Prof. Arunava Sen (sen.arunava@gmail.com) with a copy of their paper before July 1, 2015.
