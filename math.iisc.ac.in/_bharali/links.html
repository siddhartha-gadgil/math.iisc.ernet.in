  • Notes from 2010 ATM Workshop in Complex Analysis and Dynamics

    Below are some handwritten notes of 6 lectures on some basic topics in SCV, culminating in a characterisation of domains of holomorphy, that I delivered at an ATM Workshop held from March 1 to March 13, 2010.

    Warning: I make no claims that these notes are error-free (any errors, however, will be obvious and minor)! These are rapidly written notes that fill out many (but not all) of the details of the concepts, theorems, etc., that I talked about over six 80-minute lectures.
