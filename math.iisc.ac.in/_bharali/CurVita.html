ABRIDGED CURRICULUM VITAE

  • Born May 13, 1974, in Guwahati, India

  • M.Sc. (Integ.) in Mathematics from the Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur, 1997

  • Ph.D. from the University of Wisconsin-Madison, 2002

  • Assistant Professor (Visiting), Department of Mathematics, University of Michigan, 2002–2005

  • Assistant Professor, Department of Mathematics, Indian Institute of Science, Sep. 2005–Sep. 2011

  • Young Associate of the Indian Academy of Sciences, 2006–2009

  • Associate of the International Centre for Theoretical Physics, 2007–the present

  • Awarded the INSA Medal for Young Scientists, 2009

  • Tenured at the Department of Mathematics, Indian Institute of Science, April 2010

  • Associate Professor, Department of Mathematics, Indian Institute of Science, Oct. 2011–the present

  • Guest Associate Professor, Department of Mathematics, Norwegian University of Science and Technology , Trondheim, Aug. 2012–June 2013 (on sabbatical)

  • Awarded one of the 2013–2014 Swarnajayanti Fellowships in March 2015.
