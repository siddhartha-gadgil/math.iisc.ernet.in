ALGEBRAIC TOPOLOGY

 

 

 Homology : Singular homology, Excision, Mayer-Vietoris, Acyclic models, CW-complexes, Cellular homology, Homology

 with coefficients.

 

 Cohomology : Cohomology groups, relative cohomology,  Cup  product,  Kunneth formula, Ellenberg-Ziber theorem,

 Cap product, Orientation on mainfolds, Poincare duality, Lefschefz duality.

 

 Books

  • Hatcher, A., Algebraic Topology, Cambridge Univ. Press, Cambridge, 2002.

  • Greenberg, M. J., Lectures on Algebraic Topology,  W. A. Bejamin  Ind., London, 1973.

  • Munkres.,  J. R., Elements of Algebraic Topology, The Benjamin Cummings Publishing Co., Inc., Massachusetts, 1984.

  • Spanier E. H., Algebraic Topology, Tata McGraw- Hill, Bombay-New Delhi, 1966.
