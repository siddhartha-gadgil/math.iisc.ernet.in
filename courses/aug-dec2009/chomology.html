Cohomology of Manifolds and Groups

This course is a sequel to `Introduction to Algebraic Topology'

  • Review of singular homology; Cohomology groups; Cup and Cap products.
  • CW complexes, Homotopy groups and Hurewicz theorem.
  • Whitehead's theorem , obstruction theory for CW complexes
  • Poincare duality and other duality theorems, Applications.
  • Homological Algebra: Free and Projective resolutions, cohomology and homology groups of a module.
  • Cohomology of groups and applications: cohomological dimension, group actions on spheres.

    • Books

    • Hatcher, A., Algebraic topology. Cambridge University Press, Cambridge, 2002.
    • Brown, K. S., Cohomology of Groups, Springer Verlag Graduate Texts in Mathematics 87, 1982.
