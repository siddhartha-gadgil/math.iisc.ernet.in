Introduction to Several Complex Variables

Interpolation in several complex variables. Classical theorems of Nevanlinna-Pick type, Rudin-Carleson
type theorems in one and higher dimensions; d-bar methods.

Books

  • Hormander, L., Introduction to Complex Analysis in Several Variables, 3rd ed., North-Holland, 1990
  • Krantz, S. G., Function Theory of Several Complex Variables, AMS Chelsea Publishing, 2001.
  • Rudin, W., Function Theory in the Unit Ball of Cn, Springer-Verlag, 1980.
