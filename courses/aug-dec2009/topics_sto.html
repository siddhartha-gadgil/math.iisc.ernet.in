Topics in Stochastic Processes

List of topics to be covered:

1. Poisson processes.
2. Levy processes.
3. Weak convergence for point processes.
4. Local times and excursions.
5.Applications:With the above as tools, we shall study random graphs at criticality. In particular, the course will cover scaling limits for the sizes
of largest components, for both Erdos-Renyi and inhomogeneous random graphs.

References

  • Olav Kallenberg, Foundations of Modern Probability, second edition, Springer 2002.
  • Jean Bertoin, Levy Processes, Cambridge University Press 1996.
  • David Aldous, "Brownian excursions, critical random graphs and the multiplicative coalescent", The Annals of Probability, 1997, Vol. 25, No. 2.
  • Relevant current literature.
