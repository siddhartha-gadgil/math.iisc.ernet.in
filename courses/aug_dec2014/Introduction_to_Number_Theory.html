Introduction to Number Theory

Part I : Factorization and prime numbers, Congruences, Primitive roots, Quadratic residues, Continued fractions, Irrational numbers, Approximation of irrationals by rationals, Some Diophantine equations, Algebraic and Transcendental numbers.

Part II : Arithmetical functions, Averages of Arithmetical functions, Summation formulas, Distribution of primes-I (elementary estimates), Dirichlet series and Euler products, Introduction to the Riemann zeta function.

 

Recommended books

  • Hardy, G.H. and Wright, E.M., An Introduction to the Theory of Numbers (6th ed, Oxford University Press, (2008).
  • Ireland, K. and Rosen, M., A Classical Introduction to Modern Number Theory, GTM 84, Springer, (1990).
  • Apostol, T.M., Introduction to Analytic number theory, UTM, Springer, (1976). Niven I., Zuckerman, H.S. and Montgomery, H.L., An introduction to the theory of Numbers (Fifth ed.), John Wiley and Sons, Inc., (1991).
