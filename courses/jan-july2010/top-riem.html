Topics in Riemannian Geometry

The first half of the course will focus on convergence theory of Riemannian manifolds. Gromov-Hausdorff convergence, Lipschitz convergence and collapsing theory will be discussed. The second half will be about the Ricci flow. Existence and uniqueness, maximum principles and Hamilton's theorem for 3-manifolds with positive Ricci curvature will be covered.

Prerequisites:

  • Reimannian Geometry (MA 333) and Algebraic Topology (MA 332)
