THEORY OF SEMIGROUPS AND APPLICATIONS

 

 

Introduction to integration in Bananch spaces, Semigroups in B-spaces and Hille-Yoshida theorem, Examples-Heat 
semigroup in R n, sub-ordination of Semigroups, contraction semigroups and Dissipative operators, Chernoff's theorem
and Trotter-Kato product formula.
 
Applications: Mean Ergocic Theorem and Central limit theorem, Construction of Brownian motion and Feynman-Kac formula.
Markov semigroups, it's construction on L_1 and further generalization on B_1, the B-space of trace-class operators.
