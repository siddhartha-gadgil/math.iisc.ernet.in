FUNCTIONAL ANALYSIS

Basic topological concepts, Metric spaces, Normed linear spaces, Banach spaces, Bounded linear functionals and dual spaces, Hahn Banach  Theorem, Bounded linear operators, Open mapping-theorem, closed gragh theorem, Banach- steinhaus theorem, Hilbert spaces, Riesz representation theorem, Orthonormal sets,  Orthogonal complements, Bounded operators on a Hilbert space upto the spectral theorem for compact, self-adjoint operators.

Books

  • Conway, J. B., A  course in Functional Analysis, Springer, 1990.
  • Taylor, A. E., Introduction to Functional Analysis, Wiley International Edition, 1958.
  • Bachman, G., and Narici, L., Functional Analysis, Academic Press, 1966.
