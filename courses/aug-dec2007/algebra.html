Algebraic Geometry-I

Varieties: algebraic sets, Hilbert's nullstellansatz, Noetherian rings and the Hilbert Basis theorem. Affine varieties, projective varieties and varieties
in general. Properties, morphisms, products, etc. Local Properties, dimension.

Sheaves and vector bundles.

Divisor (on smooth varieties) and invertible sheaves (=line bundles); maps to projective space.

Applications of the Riemann-Roch theorem.


References:

1. Mumford, David: REd book of Varieties and Schemes LNM Math 1358.

2. Shafarevich: Basic Algebraic Geometry 1: Varieties in Projective space, 2nd ed.

3. Hartshorne, Robin: Algebraic Geometry.

4. Vakil, Ravi: Notes available on his web page: math.stanford.edu/~vak.
