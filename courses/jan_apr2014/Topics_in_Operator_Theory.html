Topics in Operator Theory

Prerequisite: A First Course in Functional Analysis

C*-algebras, Calkin algebra, Compact and Fredholm operators, Index spectral theorem, the Weyl-von Neumann-Berg Theorem and the Brown-Douglas-Fillmore Theorem.

 

Recommended books

  • Brown, L. G., Douglas, R. G., Fillmore, P.A., Unitary Equivalence Modulo the Compact Operators and Extensions of C*- Algebras,. Proceedings of a Conference on Operators Theory (Dalhousie Univ., Halifax, N. S., 1973), pp 58-128. Lecture Notes in Math., Vol. 345, Springer, Berlin, 1973.
  • Davidson, K. R., C*-algebras by Example, Fields Institute Monographs, 6, American Mathematical Society, 1996.
  • Douglas, R. G., Banach Algebra Techniques in Operator Theory, Springer Verlag, 1998.
  • Douglas, R. G., C*- algebra Extensions and K-homology. Annals of Mathematics Studies 95, Princeton University Press, 1980.
  • Murphy, G. J., C*-algebras and Operator Theory, Academic Press, 1990.
