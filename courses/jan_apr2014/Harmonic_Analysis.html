Harmonic Analysis

Prerequisites : MA 326

Harmonic Analysis in phase space: Heisenberg group, Fourier-Wigner transform, Weyl transform, Time-Frequency analysis, Short-time Fourier transform, Gabor frames, Zak transform, Wavelets and modulation spaces, Uncertainty principles.

 

Recommended books

  • Folland, G. B., A Course in Abstract Harmonic Analysis, Studies in Advanced Mathematics, CRC Press, 1995.
  • Groechenig, K., Foundations of time frequency analysis, Birkhauser, Boston (2000).
