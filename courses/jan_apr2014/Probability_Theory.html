Probability Theory

Probability measures and random variables, pi and lamda systems, Expectation, Moment-generating function, Characteristic function, Laws of large numbers, Limit theorems, Conditional contribution and expectation, Martingales, Infinitely divisible laws and stable laws.

 

Recommended books

  • Breiman, L., Probability, Addison-Wesley, 1968.
  • Laha, R. G., and Rohatgi, V. K., Probability Theory, John Wiley, 1979.
  • Borkar, V. S., Probability Theory : An Advanced Course, Springer-Verlag, 1995.
