Symplectic Geometry

Linear symplectic geometry, Symplectic and contact manifolds, Darboux's theorem, Lagrangian submanifolds, Moment maps and symplectic reduction, Symplectic and contact dynamics, An introduction to pseudoholomorphic-curve methods.

 

Recommended books

  • McDuff, D and Salamon, D., Introduction to Symplectic Topology, Oxford Mathematical Monographs, 2nd edition, 1998.
  • Cannas da Silva, A., Lectures on Symplectic Geometry, Lecture Notes in Mathematics 1764, Springer-Verlag, 2001.
  • Arnol'd, V. I., Mathematical Methods of Classical Mechanics, 2nd Edition, Springer-Verlag, 1989.
  • Geiges, H., An Introduction to Contact Topology, Cambridge University Press, 2008.
