Calculus on Manifolds

Functions of several variables, Directional derivatives and continuity, total derivativem, mean value theorem for differentiable functions, Taylor’s formula. The inverse function and implicit function theorems, extrema of functions of several variables and lagrange multipliers. Sard’s theorem. Integration on Euclidean spaces, Fubini’s theorem, the change of variables formula and partitions of unity.

Manifolds : Definitions and examples. Vector fields and differential forms on manifolds. Stokes’ theorem.
